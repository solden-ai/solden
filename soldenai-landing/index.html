Step 05 · sealed

Every step is signed and chained.

When the Box closes, the chain seals. Every transition, every override, every reversal is captured. Six months later the audit committee reconstructs the entire workflow from one record. The hash chain proves nothing was changed after the fact.

append-onlyhash-chainedSOC 2

We believe the workflow record belongs to the operator. Not to the tool that processes a slice of it.

Bronze outlasted every empire that smelted it. Workflow state should outlast every vendor that touches it. The substrate is the operator’s; the surfaces commoditize.

Tamper-evident at the schema layer.

Append-only audit log enforced by database triggers, not application code. Every event hashes the prior event. Strip a row and the chain breaks. Your auditor sees the gap.

  • guarantee no row removable, no row backdated
  • enforced db_trigger before_update, before_delete
  • algo sha256(prev_hash || event)
  • retention 7 years, configurable per region
