First shipped proof

Accounts payable, end to end.

A 70-person SaaS company

Two-person finance team. 180 invoices a month.

However an invoice arrives, ERP, e-invoicing, portal, or email, a person carries it: chases approvers in Slack, keys it into NetSuite by hand. Approvals sit for a week. Duplicates slip. Month-end is reconciliation against memory.

Solden gives each one a live record, matches it, routes the approval to the owner where they already work, posts to NetSuite on policy, keeps the trail. Cycle time drops from 17 days to under 5.
