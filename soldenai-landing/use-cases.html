Use cases

Use cases for teams whose work gets stuck between email, chat, and the ERP.

Invoices waiting on managers. Vendor setup waiting on tax and bank details. Purchase requests waiting on budget approval.

Solden helps growing companies keep the owner, next step, blocker, decision, ERP outcome, and audit trail together without asking the team to move into another dashboard.

Who feels this first

A multi-location retailer or franchise group

Store managers approve repairs, utilities, inventory, and local vendor bills, but the finance team has to chase approvals across email, chat, and spreadsheets before anything can be posted.

Typical pain
Invoices sit because the store owner, PO, or receipt is unclear.
Solden helps
Routes approval to the right location owner, tracks the wait, and posts the approved bill to ERP.
invoice arrives→store approval→controller review→ERP post

A construction, logistics, or field-service company

Subcontractor bills, materials, delivery charges, and change-order invoices need project context before finance can approve them.

Typical pain
Finance waits on project managers for job codes, receipts, or proof of work.
Solden helps
Keeps the invoice tied to the job, asks the right owner for evidence, and escalates stale waits.
vendor bill→job match→PM approval→ready to pay

A SaaS or professional-services finance team

Software renewals, contractor invoices, cloud bills, and department spend all need budget context and approval before month-end closes cleanly.

Typical pain
The CFO asks what is waiting and nobody can answer without checking five tools.
Solden helps
Shows every live invoice, owner, approval age, exception, and ERP posting outcome in one record.
renewal notice→budget check→department approval→audit trail

A nonprofit, school, clinic, or multi-entity operator

Program spend, grants, reimbursements, vendor onboarding, and entity-specific approvals need a clean record because audit questions arrive months later.

Typical pain
Approvals happen informally, proof is scattered, and the audit trail has to be rebuilt later.
Solden helps
Preserves who approved, why, what was missing, and what was posted for each entity or program.
request→program approval→finance check→proof retained

First shipped proof

Accounts payable is the first place we prove it.

An invoice lands in email. Solden reads it, checks the vendor and match, finds who has to approve, routes the decision in Slack or Teams, posts the approved bill to the ERP, and keeps the evidence on the record.

Before
AP staff chase approvers, search old threads, and re-enter approved bills into the ERP.
With Solden
The invoice has one live record with owner, blocker, approval age, policy checks, and ERP status.
Result
Finance can answer what is waiting, who owns it, what happens next, and what proof exists.
