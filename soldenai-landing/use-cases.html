Use cases

What you can run on Solden.

Declare a workflow. Its states, its approvals, who owns each step. Solden runs it.

Same runtime, every domain. The same audit trail, exception handling, and portable record for whatever you point it at.

Workflows, end to end

Accounts payable

Invoices land from email or the ERP, get matched and checked, route for approval, then post and reconcile, every step on the record.

received→ matched→ approved→ posted→ closed

Procurement & purchase orders

A request becomes a PO, runs the approval ladder by amount, then tracks receipt against what was ordered before it closes.

draft→ pending approval→ approved→ received→ closed

Contract review

A draft goes to legal, comes back approved or with changes requested, and every redline and sign-off is captured in order.

draft→ in review→ approved / changes requested

Vendor onboarding

A new supplier submits details, the right checks run, and an owner approves, with the full trail kept for audit.

submitted→ in checks→ approved / rejected

Access & provisioning requests

Someone requests access, a manager and IT approve in turn, and the grant is provisioned, every approver on the record.

requested→ manager approval→ IT approval→ granted

Expense & reimbursement approvals

A claim is submitted, reviewed against policy, approved, and reimbursed, with exceptions surfaced instead of buried.

submitted→ in review→ approved→ reimbursed
