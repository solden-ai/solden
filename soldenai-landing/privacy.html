Privacy posture · v1.0 · May 2026

What we do with your data.

What we collect. What we do with it. How long we keep it.

Who we are

Solden Technologies Ltd., a UK company building the workflow runtime for the back office. This page covers what we do with personal data you give us through soldenai.com. Customer-tenant data we process under contract is governed separately by our DPA.

What we collect

From the contact form: your name, work email, company, role, primary ERP, topic, and a short message about your back office. That is it.

From your visit: standard server logs (IP address, user agent, page, timestamp). We do not run third-party analytics that follow you across sites. No tracking pixels. No advertising cookies.

Why we collect it

To reply to your inquiry. To route the conversation to the right person. To read your situation before the first call so we are not wasting your time.

Server logs exist to keep the site up and to protect it from abuse.

Where it lives

Form submissions land in our internal CRM and the founder inbox. Server logs live with our hosting provider for a short window. We do not sell, enrich, or syndicate any of it.

Who else sees it

The Solden team. Plus the small set of sub-processors we need to run the site and our email: hosting, email delivery, form intake. We will share the current list of sub-processors on request to privacy@soldenai.com.

How long we keep it

Conversation records: while a relationship is active, plus 24 months after the last exchange in case you come back. Server logs: 90 days.

Your rights

You can ask us to show you what we hold on you, correct anything wrong, export it, or delete it. Email privacy@soldenai.com and we will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU or UK, you also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

Cookies

The site uses one first-party preference cookie to remember small UI choices. No third-party tracking. No advertising network.

Changes

If anything material shifts, we will update this page and bump the version stamp at the top. Last reviewed: 18 May 2026.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@soldenai.com. Security concerns: security@soldenai.com.
