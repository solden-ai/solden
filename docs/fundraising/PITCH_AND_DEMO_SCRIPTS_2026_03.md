# Pitch And Demo Scripts

Date: 2026-03-25

Use this file for live meetings. It keeps one narrative backbone while changing emphasis for the audience.

## Shared Backbone

Solden is the AI execution layer for finance teams. The first production skill runs AP from Gmail.

Invoices already arrive in Gmail, but finance teams still have to triage them manually, chase approvals in Slack, and then re-enter approved work into ERP. Solden handles that workflow from the inbox: process invoices, route approvals, validate against ERP, and write approved invoices back.

The immediate win is simple:

- no manual approval chasing
- no duplicate ERP entry

The broader opportunity is that the same pattern exists in other finance workflows after AP.

## Investor-First Opening

Solden is the AI execution layer for finance teams. The first production skill runs AP from Gmail. That sounds narrow, but it solves a big coordination problem that finance teams still handle manually across Gmail, Slack, spreadsheets, and ERP.

The market already acknowledges that email is part of AP. The difference is that most products treat email as intake, notification, or something to route around. We think Gmail should be the operating surface.

The wedge is AP because it is frequent, painful, inbox-native, and tied directly to ERP outcomes. If we win that workflow, we can expand into adjacent finance workflows like vendor issues, finance exceptions, reconciliation, and close coordination.

The short version is: everyone acknowledges the workflow exists, but nobody really owns it from Gmail. That is the opening.

## Operator-First Opening

Solden starts by taking the AP work you already do in Gmail and removing the worst parts of it.

Instead of watching the inbox, triaging invoices by hand, chasing people for approvals, and entering approved invoices into ERP again, Solden processes the invoice, routes the approval, checks the ERP rules, and writes the approved invoice back.

## 2-Minute Demo Script

Start in Gmail.

"This is where the work already starts. Invoices arrive here, but finance still has to move the work across multiple tools."

Open the Solden surface.

"Solden has already triaged the AP email into the right workflow or entity."

Show the invoice record or pipeline.

"Now we have the invoice data, the current state, any blockers, and the next action in one place."

Show approval routing.

"Instead of finance manually chasing approvers, Solden routes the approval through Slack or Teams."

Show ERP validation or writeback.

"Before approval or posting, Solden checks the invoice against ERP rules. Once it is approved, Solden writes it back without someone entering the same data again."

Close.

"That is the wedge: run AP from Gmail without manual approval chasing or duplicate ERP entry."

## Usage Notes

- Use the investor opening when the room cares about why this can become a company.
- Use the operator opening when the room cares about workflow pain and pilot value.
- The demo should always end on the same promise: AP from Gmail, no approval chasing, no duplicate ERP entry.
